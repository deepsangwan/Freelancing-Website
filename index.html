About

We built freelancing websites that helps match freelancers with potential employers. The jobs on the platform are sourced from all kinds of different clients, ranging from small businesses to fortune some companies..

  • 2019-2020

    Our Humble Beginnings...

    I saw a gap in the market: people, one side freelancing and businesses on the other, searching for a web designer to make their custom websites. I had no idea of creating websites that they could come in many different shapes and sizes. There were a lot of freelancers but not sufficient clientele to match with. I thought, “Hey! Would it be great if I could find those businesses?” I first thought that I'd just tell my friends about it, but quickly realized that wouldn't yield excellent results. So I thought what could I do? Maybe starting with word-of-mouth? Maybe bars? And then it hit me: online marketing! That's when Freelancing Website clicked in my mind, and now here we are!

  • March 2021

    An Agency is Born!

    We are a group of passionate people willing to help you succeed online with white-glove service. Our mission is to take your business to the next level and beyond. We do this by providing truly customized solutions that match business needs, simplify processes, and reduce expenses.

  • December 2021

    Transition to Full Service

    It's no surprise that freelancing is on the rise; over 1 billion people worldwide are pursuing freelancing jobs to supplement their income and their careers. In 2019, 1 out of 3 workers were freelancing or looking towards freelancing. By 2027, that number is expected to jump to a staggering 69%, largely due to millennials entering the workforce. But the growth of this industry has brought changes in the way clients hire freelance talent and the way freelancers apply for and manage jobs.
